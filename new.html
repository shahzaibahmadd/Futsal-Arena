ABOUT US

Elevating Sports to New Heights! Pakistan's Tallest Indoor Courts at 43 Feet.

Welcome to Futsalrange, your premier destination for cricket and football enthusiasts! With top-notch facilities at our DHA and Wapda Town locations, we offer a dynamic environment for players of all levels. Join us today and experience the thrill of sports like never before!

Futsalrange DHA features three courts, including one double court and two single courts, offering ample space for cricket and football enthusiasts to enjoy. With courts towering at 43 feet, players can unleash impressive shots and maneuvers, all on imported 40mm turf ensuring an exceptional playing experience.

Meanwhile, at Futsalrange Wapda Town, our versatile double court can be partitioned into two single courts, allowing for flexible sports activities. Standing at 30 feet high, players can aim high and enjoy optimal performance on imported 30mm turf. Whether at DHA or Wapda Town, Futsalrange promises an unmatched sports experience.
